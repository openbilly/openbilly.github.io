- 春回大地万物苏
- 开学当把懒习除
- 小子若是读书人
- 老翁自会将汝扶

Spring back to earth all things are beginning to reborn  
When school starts, get rid of laziness  
If the boy is qualified to read the book well  
The old man will help you.
